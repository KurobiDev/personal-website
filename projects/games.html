    Half-Life

    A 1998 story-driven first-person shooter developed by Valve Corporation. A game praised for it's story-driven gameplay as well the advanced features brought by the heavily modified Quake engine fork - GoldSrc. Winner of numerous "Game of the Year" awards, it even appeared on Guinness World Records as one of the best-selling games in 2008. It's legacy continues with mods being made for it to this day, some of them growing up to be a whole series, such as Counter-Strike.

    Quake

    A 1996 first-person shooter developed by id Software of legendary fame. A game revolutionary for being the first FPS game, that utilized 3D graphics. A spiritual successor of Doom series of the same developer, it's a classic, that shaped the entire industry. A game with long-lasting legacy, with many of it's solutions being found in modern games, such as Call of Duty.
