DOOM WADS

SZKOLNA.WAD

A sort-of tribute WAD for a late Polish internet celebrity, Krzysztof Kononowicz. An idea of recreating his infamous wooden house (often called "Belvedere" or "President's palace") has been in my head for a while, but due to laziness I have only managed to finally make this map in march 2025, with the mentioned celebrity's death.

The map contains an almost faithful recreation of the house in Szkolna 17 Street in Bia≈Çystok, Poland, with an outdoors area. In current state the map has only two enemies, being stand-ins for this house's former permanent residents. I plan to add more areas for this map, polish the existing ones and add a few secrets here and there.

The map was built using the MBF21 format. It was tested on GZDoom, DOOM Retro and DOOM + DOOM II sourceports.
